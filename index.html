Ava's Demon

Ava's Demon is set on distant planets allegedly 1000 years into the future, about a 15 year old girl named Ava Ire. She has the misfortune of being haunted by a demon who takes joy in manipulating Ava's actions to make her life as miserable as possible. Only later does Ava learn about her demon's true motives, and little does she know how tumultuous her life is to become.

Status: Ongoing - Hiatus until February 2018.

Length: ~2000 pages (January 2018)
